CSC 106 Final Project: "Turn Based Battle"

Gunnar Golden and I decided that we would like to create a small game in the style of turn based RPGs such as Final Fantasy and Pokemon, so that's exactly what we did!
Playing the game is very simple. First, click the start button, then choose between my bitmoji or Gunnars.
You can now play the game via the use of the arrow keys. The function of each key is represented graphically in the upper left corner of the screen.
The moves available to you will differ based on your character selection.
To win the game, defeat all three enemies!
